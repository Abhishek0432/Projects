Welcome to PG

Paying Guest (PG) accommodation is when individuals, referred to as paying guests, rent a room in someone else's home or residential property. It's a way for people to find affordable, temporary housing without committing to a long-term lease or purchasing a property.

PG accommodations typically provide basic amenities, such as a furnished room with a bed, cupboard, and sometimes a study table. Some PG accommodations may include meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner) as part of the rent.
